Prepared to Lead

      Services

Individual Coaching

Ms. Walker specializes in customized one-on-one coaching, focusing on objectives established mutually between the individual and the employer. Her coaching programs prepare newly promoted leaders to enhance their skills in areas such as:

  • Leadership presence
  • Strategic thinking and goal setting
  • Communication
  • Supervising
  • Delegation
  • Hiring

Ms. Walker has worked extensively with clients in a wide array of industries, including financial services, biotechnology, engineering, consulting, and information technology.

Group Training

Ms. Walker also designs and delivers customized group training programs aimed at improving management and leadership skills. Past programs have included Managing People, Developing Leadership Presence, Conflict Management and Talent Selection. All of her programs are customized to meet the specific needs of her clients.
